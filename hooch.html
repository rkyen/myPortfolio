This project was my first step into design! For my Software Engineering Junior Lab, the class decided to work on an application that would serve as a drunk texting prevention tool.

I took the initiative to be the designe the app where I created a high fidelty mockup of the storyboard utilizing Sketch 3. The logo behind the design is influenced from the Bernese Mountain dog that was said to save individuals who were trapped in the mountains. The name "Hooch" was influenced from a slang word for moonshine. The color influence came from a tequlia-sunrise drink.
