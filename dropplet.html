Dropplet

Water Conservation Gaming App

DandyHacks @University of Rochester â€“ Spring 2016

This was my first Hackathon! For this project, I designed the logo, branding, and website to teach the users about the game. I was able to create website in relation for the project.

Dropplet was designed to be a game where the users utilizing the accelerometer on your Android phone where you will control the Dropplet character from your phone and watch the character move on your Chrome browser. The story stems from the idea where the dropplet falls from the shower and starts free fall until it reaches the drain with the fastest time.

The hopes for this game is to remind users about the amount of water and time they are spending when showering.
